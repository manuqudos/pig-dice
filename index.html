Let's play Dice

How to play the game;

  • Pig is game for 2 to 6 Players.
  • Players take turns rolling a dice as many times as they like.
  • If a roll is 2, 3, 4, 5 or 6, the player adds that many points to their score for the turn.
  • A player may choose to end their turn at any time and 'bank' their points.
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose all their unbanked points and their turn is over.
  • The first player to score the target or more wins.
